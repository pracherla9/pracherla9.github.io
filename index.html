Energy Grid Analytics LLC

My name is Pavan Racherla, and I am a freelance energy systems engineer. I make electric grid planning and operations software—e.g., for economic dispatch; the flow of electric power in transmission and distribution networks; and the integration of distributed energy resources like solar and wind. My tool-of-choice is mathematical optimization using JuMP. I bundle together all code and dependencies as “containerized” applications using Docker.

By way of background, I have a PhD in Engineering & Public Policy from Carnegie Mellon University. I was employed thereafter as a Postdoctoral Research Scientist at NASA GISS and then as a Research Assistant Professor in Electrical Engineering at University of Vermont.
